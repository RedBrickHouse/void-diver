Above & below

Two worlds, one loop

Run a shop on the surface. Send divers into the abyss below. One feeds the other.

Above

The shop

BALUSHA sits on the surface. Display your relics, hire clerks, and haggle with VIPs — the takings bankroll your next descent.

Below

The dive

The abyss waits beneath. Send divers into ever-changing dungeons, face what lurks there, and bring the relics back alive.

↕

Gameplay

Core gameplay

01

Three-player co-op

Team up with up to three players and conquer the dungeons — pure co-op, PvE only.

02

Dungeons that change

The dungeon wears a different face every run. A memorized route won’t save you.

03

Zone anomalies

Random anomalies sweep through the dungeon like weather, affecting divers and monsters alike.

Dungeon threats

Monsters of the abyss

Mimics that pose as chests, vicious elite monsters, and multi-phase bosses like the Soul Collector wait throughout the dungeons.
